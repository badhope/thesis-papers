While the Company believes its pricing, discount structure and supply chain monitoring controls are sufficient, if we are unable to maintain or improve upon the gross profit margins delivered in the fourth quarter of 2022, if inflationary increases continue without corresponding increases in our pricing, or if such reduced discounting and price increases cause a material impact on demand, DIRTT’s results of operations and financial condition could be adversely impacted.
