We have, at times, experienced some supply disruptions impacting certain printed products in our Promotional Solutions segment. We continue to closely monitor our supply chain to avoid delays or disruptions. We have also experienced labor supply issues in certain portions of our business. The disruptions to some regional financial institutions earlier in the year had no impact on our business or results of operations. We do not bank with any of the directly affected financial institutions, and they collectively represent an immaterial portion of our revenue. Additionally, we have very little customer concentration risk, and we believe our diversified customer base positions us well going forward.
