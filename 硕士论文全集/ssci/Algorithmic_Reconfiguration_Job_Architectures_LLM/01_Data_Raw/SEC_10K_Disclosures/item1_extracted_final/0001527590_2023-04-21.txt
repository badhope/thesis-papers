Stock Ownership Guidelines The Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee believes that stock ownership by our independent directors and certain of our executive officers is important in order to further align the interests of these individuals with those of our stockholders and expects these individuals to acquire significant ownership of equity in the Company (“Company Equity”). Our board of directors adopted minimum equity ownership guidelines for our independent directors requiring each independent director to maintain a minimum number of shares of Common Stock having a market value equal to or greater than a multiple of such independent director’s annual cash retainer (excluding any portion of the retainer fee representing additional compensation for being a committee chairman).
