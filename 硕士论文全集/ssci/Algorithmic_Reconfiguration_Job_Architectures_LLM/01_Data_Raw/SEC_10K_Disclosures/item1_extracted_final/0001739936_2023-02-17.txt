Human Capital Resources At Momentive, we are on a mission to Power the Curious. Our employee value proposition is to be: A Place Where the Curious Come to Grow and our values guide us every day. Our goal is to create an environment where everyone—no matter their background—can succeed, feel a sense of belonging, and learn from one another.
