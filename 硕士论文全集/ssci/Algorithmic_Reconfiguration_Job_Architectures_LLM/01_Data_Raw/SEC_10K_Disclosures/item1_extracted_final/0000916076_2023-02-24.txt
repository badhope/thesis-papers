Industry Risk Factors Our business depends on activity within the construction industry, which can be cyclical Economic and political uncertainty can impede growth in the markets in which we operate. If economic conditions worsen, a recession in the construction industry may occur and affect the demand for our products. Construction spending can also be disrupted by terrorist activity and armed conflicts.
