Distribution We distribute our products through a direct-store-delivery (“DSD”) distribution system and a warehouse delivery system. The warehouse delivery system involves primarily delivering our products to customers’ warehouses. The company has sold the majority of the distribution rights to market certain brands within a geographic territory to independent distributors under long-term financing arrangements.
