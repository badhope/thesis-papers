Our solid oxide stacks in electrolysis mode split water into hydrogen and oxygen using supplied carbon-free electricity. The hydrogen can be stored as compressed gas, creating the ability to produce a virtually limitless supply. ‚Äã The largest factor in the cost of electrolysis-produced hydrogen is the cost of electricity.
