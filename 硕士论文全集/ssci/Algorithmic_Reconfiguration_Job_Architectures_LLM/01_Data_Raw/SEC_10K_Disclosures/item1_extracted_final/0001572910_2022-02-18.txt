Human Capital We are managed and operated by the executive officers of our General Partner with oversight provided by its Board of Directors. Neither we nor our subsidiaries have any employees and as such, there are no human capital objectives or measures material to an understanding of our business. Our General Partner has the sole responsibility for providing the employees and other personnel necessary to conduct our operations. As of December 31, 2021, Phillips 66 employed approximately 600 people who provided direct support for our operations.
