The Formula Agreement contains a choice of venue clause limiting jurisdiction to courts in Tel-Aviv-Jaffa, Israel. The Service Agreement’s term is unlimited, but it may be terminated by either party upon providing a written notice of termination 180-day prior; any such termination shall not detract from the validity of the option to purchase the Formulas.
