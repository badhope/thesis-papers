The pipeline, storage and terminal systems that are operated by SPLC are controlled from a central control room located in Houston, Texas. The Operating Company, on behalf of Triton, engaged SPLC to operate the Norco Assets pursuant to an operating agreement, and such assets are operated by SPLC through the provision of services by employees assigned by SOPUS and located at the facilities under the terms of an employee assignment and services level agreement between SOPUS and SPLC.
