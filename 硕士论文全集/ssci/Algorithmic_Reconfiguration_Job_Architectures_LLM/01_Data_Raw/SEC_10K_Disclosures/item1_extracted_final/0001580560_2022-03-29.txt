Factors outside of our control will affect enrollments and tuition costs, including the following: • Reduced enrollment in higher education due to lack of funding, increases to cost of attendance or other inflationary pressure. Significant reductions in student funding, through grants or loans, may reduce enrollments and decrease the payment volume we process.
