The Company is developing two proprietary, first-line stimulant medications, CTx-1301 (dexmethylphenidate) and CTx-1302 (dextroamphetamine), for the treatment of ADHD intended for all patient segments: children, adolescents, and adults. The Company is preparing to start Phase 3 clinical trials for CTx-1301 in the second quarter of 2022.
