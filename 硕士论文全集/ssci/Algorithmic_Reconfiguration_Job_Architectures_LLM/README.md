## Research Highlights

- **Theoretical Contribution:** Dynamic job architecture theory integrating signaling theory with AI paradox literature
- **Methodological Approach:** Triple identification strategy (TWFE + entropy balancing + instrumental variable)
- **Data:** 20,609 firm-year observations from SEC 10-K disclosures (2020-2024)
- **Key Finding:** Inverted-U moderation pattern of digital infrastructure on AI-collaborative task signals
